FLASH

About

Flash interface can be accessed by a registered user.Its interface will allow user to Start,Create,Edit or Delete from their Dashboards.Start Deck will allow user to self evaluate.Flash will display front of a deck as you start a deck and back will be revealed as you hover over the card.Upon reveal the card prompts three buttons 'Easy', 'Medium' and 'Hard' depending on the difficulty of the card according to the user.
The scoring for these button clicks is:
Easy: +5 points
Medium: +3 points
Hard: +1 point
